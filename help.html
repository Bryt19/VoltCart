Orders & Payments

How do I place an order on VoltCart?
+
Simply browse our product catalog, click "Add to Cart" on items you want, then open your cart by clicking the cart icon in the navbar. Review your items, click "Proceed to Checkout", fill in your delivery details, and complete payment securely via Paystack.
What payment methods are accepted?
+
We accept all major payment methods through Paystack — including debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), and bank transfers. All transactions are processed in Ghanaian Cedis (GH₵).
Is my payment information secure?
+
Absolutely. VoltCart uses Paystack for all payment processing. Paystack is PCI-DSS compliant and uses 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details on our servers.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
+
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. Contact our support team immediately via email with your transaction reference number. Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does delivery take?
+
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days within Ghana. Express delivery (same-day or next-day) is available for orders placed before 2PM in Accra and major cities. Delivery times may vary slightly for remote areas.
How much does shipping cost?
+
Our flat-rate shipping fee is GH₵3.99 per order, regardless of the number of items. Free shipping is available on orders above GH₵500.
Do you ship outside Ghana?
+
Currently, VoltCart ships exclusively within Ghana. We are actively working on expanding to other West African countries. Subscribe to our newsletter to be notified when international shipping becomes available.

Products & Warranty

Are VoltCart products genuine/authentic?
+
Yes — every product sold on VoltCart is 100% authentic and sourced directly from verified manufacturers and authorized distributors. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods.
What warranty do products come with?
+
All products carry a minimum 1-year manufacturer warranty. Premium products (headphones, smart watches, keyboards) include our exclusive VoltCart 2-Year Warranty at no extra cost. See individual product pages for specific warranty terms.
