Warranty Coverage

Every VoltCart product comes with our industry-leading warranty protection — because quality should last.

Standard Products

1 Year

Speakers, desk accessories, cables, and peripherals

Premium Products

2 Years

Headphones, smart watches, keyboards, and high-end peripherals

VoltCart Extended

3 Years

Available as an add-on at checkout for select flagship products

What's Covered

Manufacturing defects
Hardware malfunctions
Faulty components
Battery defects (within 12 months)
Display/screen issues
Connectivity failures

What's Not Covered

  • Physical damage from drops, impacts, or spills
  • Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Normal wear and tear (scratches, cosmetic fading)
  • Damage from improper use or storage
  • Products with removed or tampered serial numbers

How to Claim Warranty

To make a warranty claim, please:

  • Email us with your original order reference and a description of the issue
  • Attach photos or a short video clearly showing the defect
  • Our team will evaluate your claim within 2 business days
  • Approved claims will receive a replacement or repair at no cost
