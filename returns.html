Support

Returns & Refunds

Hassle-free returns within 30 days. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

30-Day Return Window: You have 30 full days from the date of delivery to return any item for a full refund or exchange — no questions asked, as long as the product is in its original condition.

How to Return an Item

1

Contact Us

Email support with your order reference and reason for return.

2

Get Approved

We'll review your request and send a return authorization within 24 hours.

3

Ship It Back

Package the item securely and drop it off at any courier partner location.

4

Get Refunded

Refund is processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.

Eligible Items for Return

Items must meet the following conditions to be eligible for return:

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Item is unused and in its original condition
  • Original packaging, accessories, and documentation included
  • Product seal is unbroken (for sealed items)

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper handling
  • Products with tampered or removed serial numbers
  • Software or digital products after download
  • Items returned after the 30-day window

Refund Timeline

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed as follows:

  • Paystack (Card/Mobile Money): 3–5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 5–7 business days
  • You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund has been issued.
